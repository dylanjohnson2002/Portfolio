About Me

Hello, my name is Dylan Johnson, and I'm currently embracing the exciting world of gaming and interactive media in my second year at Boise State's GIMM program. With a burning passion for game design, I am enthusiastically diving into the intricacies of creating engaging and immersive gaming experiences. Outside the digital realm, I find joy and fulfillment in my job at a local pet store, where I dedicate my time to caring for animals. This blend of technological creativity and a love for animals defines who I am and continuously shapes my journey both personally and professionally.

Hobbies

I grew up in Alaska, a place known for its stunning landscapes and abundant wildlife. It's there that I developed a passion for hunting and fishing, activities that allowed me to connect with nature in a profound way. The tranquility of the wilderness has always been a big part of my life. In addition to my outdoor pursuits, I also love video games. I love immersing myself in different types of video games, from action-packed adventures to strategic challenges. Gaming is a great way to unwind and experience exciting new worlds. My love for animals extends beyond the great outdoors. At home, I maintain several aquariums, which are a source of relaxation and enjoyment. Watching the fish swim and caring for the aquatic plants brings a sense of peace and fulfillment to my daily life.
